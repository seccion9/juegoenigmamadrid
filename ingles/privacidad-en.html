COOKIES

Some sections of this website use cookies (small information files that the server sends to the computer of the person accessing the page). The purpose of a cookie is to improve the service when the user returns to the website. By collecting information about your preferences, the website can personalize your experience when you reconnect; they also help improve the website by identifying which sections are most visited, which allows Room Escape Formacion to enhance its service. You can configure your browser to notify you when a cookie is requested or to reject them completely. Accepting cookies does not, under any circumstances, give Room Escape Formacion access to your computer or personal information.

Cookies will be stored on your computer's hard drive until you delete them. You can configure your browser to notify you when cookies are present or to automatically reject them. If you reject cookies, you can still use the Website, although this may limit your access to some features or prevent the Website from functioning properly. When you access the website, the system will notify you of the presence of cookies; by continuing to browse the website, you are accepting their use.
