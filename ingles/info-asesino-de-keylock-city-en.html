WHAT IS A HALL ESCAPE?

Hall Escape games are specially designed for large groups .

NIt's not an Escape Room, although they may seem similar. An Escape Room has a limit of six participants per room, a much smaller number than that allowed by a Hall Escape.

We call Hall Escape a multiplayer game where the goal is not to escape from a room. The entire plot unfolds in the same space so all players participate together.

We will continue to use logic, ingenuity, observation, skill, and teamwork as in a classic escape game, but with a much more social and group component.

Hall Escape has nothing to envy in an escape room,, They are different concepts but equally fun, exciting, and challenging !The hour will fly by!
